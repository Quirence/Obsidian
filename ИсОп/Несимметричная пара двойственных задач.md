\left\{\begin{array}{}Ax = b \\
x \ge 0\end{array}\right.
\end{array}$$
$$\begin{array}{} G(y) = b^Ty \rightarrow max \\
\begin{array}{}A^Ty \le C \end{array}
\end{array}$$
